Happy Teacher's Day


To the brother who lights the path I tread,
With wisdom’s flame and heart so wide,
In every field, by every thread,
You’ve been my guide, my trusted guide.

On this day of teachers’ praise, I honor you for all you give,
Your knowledge spreads in countless ways, And teaches me how best to live.
With patience vast and insight clear, You share your world with open hand,
In every lesson, far and near, You help me rise, you help me stand.
So here’s to you, my dear mentor, On this Teacher’s Day,
I say, Your guidance is a treasure near, And fills my heart in every way.
 - By Your One & Only Sister
