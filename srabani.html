Happy Teacher's Day






On Teacher’s Day, we sing your praise,
For guiding us through tech’s vast maze.
In bytes and code, your wisdom gleams,
You shape our futures, fuel our dreams.

With every lesson, crisp and clear,
You make complex things disappear.
Through algorithms, loops, and lines,
You help us see the grand designs.

In every keystroke, every click,
You teach us tricks both neat and slick.
Your patience shines, your knowledge flows,
In digital realms, your brilliance shows.

So here’s to you, with gratitude,
For every insight you’ve imbued.
On this day, we celebrate your way,
And thank you for all you convey.
- By Sneha Ghosh
