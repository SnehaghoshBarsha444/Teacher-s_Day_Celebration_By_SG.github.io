Happy Teacher's Day



On Teacher’s Day, we lift our voice,
For you, whose math we all rejoice.
In numbers’ dance and formulas’ play,
You guide us through each complex way.
With every proof and problem set,
You spark a light we won’t forget.
Your wisdom makes the abstract clear,
Turning doubts to answers near.
In algebra’s grace,
You’ve shown us how to find our place.
Each lesson’s depth and clarity,
Reflects your skill and generosity.

To you, our guide, so wise and true,
We celebrate the work you do.
For shaping minds and hearts with care,
Your impact is beyond compare.
- By Sneha Ghosh
