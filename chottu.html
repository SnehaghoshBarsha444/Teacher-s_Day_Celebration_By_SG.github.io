Happy Teacher's Day


To Kakai, a guide and cherished friend,
Your wisdom flows where shadows blend.
In life's vast sea, you steer my course,
A beacon bright, a steadfast force.

Through trials faced and dreams we chase,
Your calm assures a gentle grace.
With every step and counsel shared,
A guide who's always truly cared.

Your laughter warms the coldest night,
Your stories turn the dark to light.
In every word and deed so wise,
I see the truth within your eyes.

Kakai, you are the sage I trust,
In you, a mentor’s heart is just.
With gratitude and love so true,
I honor all I’ve learned from you.
- By Sneha Ghosh (Barsha)
