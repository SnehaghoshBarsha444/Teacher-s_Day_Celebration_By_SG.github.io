Happy Teacher's Day!



You are the light that guides me through,
The one who helps me learn and grow.
You teach me things I never knew,
And help me face the world below.

You inspire me to reach for the stars,
And chase my dreams with all my might.
You help me heal my deepest scars,
And make everything seem bright.

I'm grateful for all that you do,
And for the person that you are.
I hope this poem conveys to you,
How much I respect you, dear teacher.


- By Sneha Ghosh
