Only For You


In every dawn, your light ignites the day,
A friend so true, in every single way,
With laughter shared and sorrows gently borne,
You’ve been a guide from every dusk till dawn.

Your wisdom’s deep, like oceans vast and clear,
In every word, your thoughtful heart appears,
With every moment spent, your worth is shown,
A friend whose loyalty has ever grown.

In trials faced and triumphs yet to come,
You stand beside me, where I’m never numb.
Respect and trust define the bond we share,
A precious gift, beyond compare.

Your spirit lifts me when the shadows fall,
A beacon bright, you answer every call.
In you, I find a love that’s pure and rare,
A friend whose presence fills the very air.

For all you are and all you give so kind,
A treasure rare, in you I always find.
My heartfelt thanks and admiration true,
Dear friend, forever cherished, just for you.

Through seasons’ change and as the years unfold,
Our bond remains a story yet untold.
In every chapter, you are ever near,
A friend whose grace will ever persevere.
- By Sneha Ghosh
