Only For You




To you, my friend, whose heart is pure and true,
In every moment, I find strength in you.
Your kindness wraps me in a warm embrace,
And lifts me up with gentle, steady grace.



In laughter shared and in the quiet times,
Your wisdom shines through words and subtle rhymes.
Respect and trust in every gesture gleam,
A friendship built upon a timeless dream.



Through highs and lows, your presence is a guide,
A treasured soul who stands right by my side.
For all you are, and all you always give,
I honor you, dear Rio, and cherish how we live.
- By Sneha Ghosh
