Happy Teacher's Day



In classrooms where the bright lights gleam,
You’ve been the spark behind each dream,
With gentle words and patient care,
You’ve guided us through every layer.



Your lessons weave a tapestry,
Of knowledge deep and empathy,
In every challenge, every test,
You’ve taught us how to do our best.



Through highs and lows, you’ve stood so near,
A mentor, friend, always sincere,
Your impact grows with every day,
In hearts and minds, you light the way.
- By Sneha Ghosh
