Happy Teacher's Day



As I step into this world anew,
With dreams and doubts, both fresh and true,
Your wisdom guides me through each class,
A beacon in the shadows cast.



From textbooks old to concepts bright,
You lead us with a patient light,
Though new I am to this grand stage,
Your guidance helps me turn the page.



 On this Teacher's Day, I want to say,
Your care and knowledge light the way,
With gratitude, my heart’s profound,
For all the wisdom that I’ve found.
- By Sneha Ghosh
