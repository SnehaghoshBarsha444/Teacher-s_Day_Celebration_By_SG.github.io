Homage to My Guiding Light


In sacred halls where wisdom blooms,
Your presence brightens all the rooms.
A beacon through the darkest night,
Your guidance turns the shadows bright.

In your gaze, both firm and kind,
I find my strength, my heart aligned.
Each lesson shared, a treasured spark,
Ignites my soul, dispels the dark.

Your wisdom helps my spirit grow,
In your care, seeds of kindness sow.
More than books, your truths impart
A path of honor, a steadfast heart.



Though stern at times, your love is clear,
A steady hand, a voice I revere.
How blessed am I, in fleeting time,
To walk with you, my guiding rhyme.

- By A Grateful Student
